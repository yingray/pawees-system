PAWEES 2017 International Conference

November 9-10, 2017
Taichung, Taiwan


The objective of PAWEES 2017 is to provide an interactive platform for researchers, practitioners, policy makers, and other professionals in the field of paddy and water environment from around the globe to present and share their novel advances, research findings, perspectives, and experiences to foster sustainable water and environmental management.
General session
The scope of the PAWEES 2017 Conference is on Sustainable Water and Environmental Management. Technical programme comprises the following topics:
Topic 1 | Sustainable development with respect to water and ecology
Topic 2 | Harmonizing rural development with water resources management
Topic 3 | Sustainable paddy farming
Topic 4 | Integrated watershed management
Topic 5 | Droughts and flood disaster risk management

Student session
“Sustainable Water and Environmental Management”
The abstract submission process for PAWEES 2017 will be through an online submission portal. Authors are requested to create an account and submit their abstracts for review.

Oral presentation
Time allotment for each oral presentation will be 25 minutes, including 20 minutes for presentation and 5 minutes for Q & A. Session rooms are equipped with standard PowerPoint presentation facilities.

Poster presentation
All posters should be made in A1 size. Poster boards will be provided during the poster session.
IMPORTANT DATES
Abstract Submission Deadline August 31, 2017
Notification of Acceptance September 30, 2017
Registration Deadline September 30, 2017
REGISTRATION FEES
International participants
The standard registration fee is 200 US dollars (or equivalently, 6,400 Taiwan dollars). For students and accompanying persons, the registration fee is 100 US dollars (or 3,200 Taiwan dollars). The registration fee covers conference kit (program book and proceedings), admission to opening ceremony, plenary sessions, scientific sessions, break refreshments, lunch, reception banquet, and field trip. Hotel accommodation and related travel costs are not included in the registration fee. 		Local participants
For international participants, registration fee will be paid on site. Payment can only be made by cash in either US dollars or Taiwan dollars. Please note that we do NOT accept credit card payment of the registration fee.
